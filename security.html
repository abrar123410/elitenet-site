Cybersecurity Tips

Stay safe online with these beginner-friendly cybersecurity practices:

  • Use strong passwords and 2FA: Combine letters, numbers, and symbols, and enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
  • Avoid phishing emails: Don’t click suspicious links or download unknown attachments.
  • Keep software updated: Install updates regularly to patch security vulnerabilities.
  • Use antivirus and firewalls: Protect your system from viruses, malware, and unauthorized access.
