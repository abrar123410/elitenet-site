Quick Tip: Improving Your Home Wi-Fi

Struggling with weak Wi-Fi signals? Make sure your router is placed in a central location, away from walls and large metal objects. For bigger homes, consider adding Wi-Fi range extenders or mesh systems. Secure your network with a strong password to avoid unauthorized access.
